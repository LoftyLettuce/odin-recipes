Air Fryer Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf and mashed potatoes are a must in the cold month of January. This air fryer meatloaf recipe is the one to make when you want it on the table quickly. It takes about a third of the time a traditional meatloaf takes, and it never turns out dry.

Ingredients

  • bread crumbs
  • whole milk
  • ground round
  • yellow onion
  • egg
  • chopped fresh thyme
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Soften breadcrumbs in milk, then mix with beef, onion, egg, and seasonings in a bowl.
  2. Form the beef mixture into two small meatloaves.
  3. Make the glaze.
  4. Cook until browned, brushing evenly with glaze toward the end of cooking time.
